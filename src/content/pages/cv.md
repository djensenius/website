## Education

- **2005–2008** — Master of Arts, Music Composition. Wesleyan University,
  Middletown CT.
- **1997–2001** — Bachelor of Science, Computer Science. Millersville University,
  Millersville PA.

## Professional Experience

### 2008–Present — Composer / Media Artist

- Build media art pieces and sound compositions using a variety of technologies
  and custom-built software.
- Created FoundSounds — a worldwide social network and collaborative art project.
- Compositions and art have been displayed internationally and throughout Canada
  (see Performances / Exhibitions below).
- Technology used: ReactJS, NodeJS, Ruby on Rails, Swift, GraphQL, PostgreSQL,
  MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, AWS, Objective-C, AngularJS, SuperCollider, Processing,
  PHP.
- Many projects are open source at
  [github.com/djensenius](https://github.com/djensenius).

### 2016–2018 — Developer, Shopify. Waterloo ON

- Worked on the Shopify Flow team — an automation tool and visual programming
  language for merchants to customize their stores and automate their supply
  management chains — from a rough concept to a shipping product.
- First engineer on the Flow project; made the decision to use ReactJS for the
  front end.
- Agile process; used Google-style design sprints to create and iterate Flow.
- Created the Sound section of the Shopify design documentation.
- Organized a Canadian Blood Services drive, concerts, and a movie screening to
  contribute to the cultural offerings at Shopify.
- Trained, mentored, and evaluated student interns.
- Technology used: ReactJS, Ruby on Rails, MySQL, GraphQL.

### 2016 — Developer, Boltmade (acquired by Shopify). Waterloo ON

- Worked in small teams to build a parking enforcement web app with Xerox PARC.
- Participated in design sprints using an agile-like working style.
- Technology used: NodeJS, AngularJS, .NET.

### 2011–2016 — Director of Technology, Social Psychology Network. Middletown CT

- Managed a suite of websites aimed at promoting peace and social justice through
  social psychology, with over 320 million impressions by the end of 2015.
- Created and managed educational web-based tools about sexism, racism, and other
  forms of prejudice and discrimination.
- Worked with world-renowned social psychologists such as Philip Zimbardo, Susan
  Fiske, and David Dunning to preserve their work to the web.
- Created tools used in classrooms and for homework assignments internationally.
- On call for emergencies; managed a small team of developers and a team of
  student interns and testers; determined technologies for future development.
- Technology used: PHP, NodeJS, MySQL, Objective-C, Perl, Shell Script.

### 2015 — Curator, New Music at the Boathouse. Kitchener ON

- Curated established and emerging artists for a monthly experimental music
  series.
- Created and edited a podcast and YouTube series; arranged travel and
  accommodations as needed.

### 2011 — Curator, New Music at the Gallery. Kitchener-Waterloo Art Gallery, Kitchener ON

- Co-curated established and emerging artists for a series of four experimental
  music concerts.

### 2001–2010 — Developer, Social Psychology Network. Middletown CT

- Programmed and maintained multiple custom-built websites; on call for
  emergencies.
- Technology used: PHP, NodeJS, MySQL, Objective-C, Perl, Shell Script.

### 2008 — Guest Curator, framework radio broadcast and podcast. London, UK

- Created a one-hour radio program based on the _Crooked River in First and Third
  Persons_ sound installation.

### 2008 — Guest Curator, Monkeytown. Brooklyn NY
