A sound installation in eight channels. Listeners are able to hear a sonic
representation of satellites currently over them.

Each group of satellites (Weather, Military, etc.) is given a unique sonic identity.
The eight channels provide an experience to the listener that allows them to
approximate the location of the satellite.

This installation can be made site-specific to anywhere in the world.
