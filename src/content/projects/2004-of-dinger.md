_of Dinger_ [1100274086–1100275383]

A piece based on the translation of GPS data for two instruments. This piece uses
Glockenspiel and Soprano. The pitch and rhythm of the first instrument are based on
coordinates and speed. Vocals are based on height, direction travelled, and speed.

Shannon "Dinger" Aldinger provided the GPS data.
