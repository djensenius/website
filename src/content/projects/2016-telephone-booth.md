A phone booth with an older-style rotary pay phone is placed in a surprising location.
The instructions plate reads:

> **FREE CALLS TODAY**\
> Dial 1 to leave a message\
> Dial 2 to retrieve a message

When someone dials 1 to leave a message they are prompted with a provocative question.
When dialling 2, a random answer is presented without the context of the original
question, creating an emergent dialogue.

Presented for CAFKA 2016.
