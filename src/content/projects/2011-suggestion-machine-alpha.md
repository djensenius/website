Collage-based artificial intelligence work using live and pre-recorded material. Using
predetermined data about different pieces, a computer creates a collage and instructs
the conductor when to execute different actions. The conductor can disagree with the
computer, at which time the computer either regenerates the rest of the piece or
refuses to and insists the conductor follow the initial instructions.
