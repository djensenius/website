By Lauren Burr, David Jensenius & Mark Prier, using HATengine.

HATFinder is an augmented-reality audio-based game, built using HATengine.

> You have arrived in the area where Special Agent Z was last seen. We have reason to
> believe that we can solve his disappearance by investigating Historical Auditory
> Traces (HATs) found in the area.
>
> Your personal communications device has been outfitted with the latest HATFinder,
> but you'll need to be discreet when using it. Plug in your headphones, put away your
> device, and listen up. Follow the Traces, but stay within signalling range. If you
> hear static, you've gone too far.
>
> And remember: this is a matter of national security. Don't screw up.

Designed for the UCLA campus. Works everywhere.
