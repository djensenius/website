---
title: Emoticon Music
year: 2005
summary: A packet sniffer sonifies emoticons crossing a network to reveal a location's mood.
tags:
  - composition
  - network
  - data
---

Emoticons are popular ways to express a certain feeling in internet emails or instant
messages. In this piece, a packet sniffer watches for emoticons and then plays an
assigned sound for each one as it comes across the network. The listener can hear the
overall mood of a certain location.

_Note: this piece should not be performed without receiving the consent of the people
on the network that their traffic will be monitored._
