_Untitled_ (2020) is a graphical score for open instrumentation. Its seven pages move through a rising arc of intensity before settling into a sparse, reflective close. Shapes carry familiar sonic identities from page to page, and each page can be read in any direction — cumulatively, or in whatever combination the performer feels fits.

Approximate length is 3:30, with each page lasting roughly twenty to forty seconds. The first six pages should build a feeling of growing intensity; the last should feel somber and reflective.

Page directions:

1. (Un)surprising
2. This keeps happening
3. A response
4. An escalation
5. Inevitable destruction
6. What's left
7. Sparse; reflective

Performers are welcome to realize and interpret the score — if you perform it, please get in touch. Licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.
