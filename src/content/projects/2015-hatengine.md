By Lauren Burr, David Jensenius & Mark Prier.

HATengine is a tool for designing and building augmented-reality sound games, sound
walks, or locative sound installations.

Players put on headphones and walk to explore the sonic landscape you've created.
Games can be changed on the fly, with updates being automatically downloaded to users
in real time — no need for annoying app updates.

HATengine will use almost any sound file. Whether it's a WAV, AIFF, or MP3, we have
you covered. You can even use Pd files.

Try HATFinder, our demo game built for UCLA. Available for Android and iOS.
